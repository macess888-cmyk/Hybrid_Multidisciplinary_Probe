# Relationship Lens

## Purpose

Inspect dependencies, boundaries, handoffs, upstream and downstream obligations, and whether one layer silently inherits the properties of another.

## Questions

- What depends on what?
- Which layer operates upstream?
- Which layer operates downstream?
- What is transferred across the boundary?
- What is lost during handoff?
- Which responsibilities remain local?
- Are claims, evidence, authority, and execution kept separate?
- Are two systems complementary or duplicative?
- Is one system claiming to replace another without evidence?
- What relationships remain unresolved?

## Pressure Tests

- connection != integration
- integration != authority transfer
- shared terminology != shared architecture
- upstream validity != downstream validity
- local correctness != system correctness
- interoperability != common truth
