# Uncertainty Lens

## Purpose

Inspect missing evidence, unresolved conditions, ambiguity, model limits, and whether uncertainty is preserved honestly.

## Questions

- What is known?
- What is inferred?
- What is assumed?
- What remains unknown?
- Is uncertainty quantified or described?
- Is missing evidence being treated as absence?
- Is uncertainty being hidden by confident language?
- What would resolve the HOLD?
- What conditions remain inaccessible?
- Is the conclusion proportional to the evidence?

## Pressure Tests

- unknown != false
- unavailable environment != product failure
- incomplete evidence != fraud
- uncertainty != weakness
- HOLD != rejection
- confidence != proof
