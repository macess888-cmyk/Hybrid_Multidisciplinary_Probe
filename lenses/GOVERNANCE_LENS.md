# Governance Lens

## Purpose

Inspect the structures that define responsibility, authority, admissibility, challenge, correction, and consequence.

## Questions

- Who defines the governing rule?
- Who may apply it?
- Who may challenge it?
- What evidence is required?
- What happens when evidence is incomplete?
- What happens when the rule is wrong?
- Is there a correction route?
- Is there independent review?
- Is accountability traceable?
- Can governance remain operational under dispute?

## Pressure Tests

- policy != governance
- oversight != authority
- monitoring != intervention
- information governance != execution governance
- governance record != governed consequence
- complaint received != correction initiated
