# Temporal Lens

## Purpose

Inspect time, sequence, freshness, delay, supersession, re-entry, and whether conclusions remain valid as conditions change.

## Questions

- When was the observation made?
- When did the evidence become available?
- Was the evidence current at decision time?
- What happened before the warning arrived?
- What changed after the decision?
- Did authority remain valid?
- Was supersession append-only?
- Was history preserved?
- Was revalidation performed?
- Did prior validity carry forward improperly?

## Pressure Tests

- later evidence cannot govern earlier consequence
- current record != historical governing record
- alert withdrawn != recovery complete
- previous validity != current validity
- supersession != historical rewriting
- delay != absence
