# Authority Lens

## Purpose

Inspect who or what possesses authority, how that authority is established, what it applies to, and how it ends.

## Questions

- Who possesses authority?
- What is the source of that authority?
- Is authority current?
- Is authority valid for this object, action, time, and scope?
- Can authority be independently demonstrated?
- Does authority transfer, expire, suspend, or revoke?
- Is apparent authority being confused with actual authority?
- Does evidence support authority, or merely describe it?
- What happens when authority is challenged?
- Can authority bind consequence?

## Pressure Tests

- title != authority
- policy != authority
- identity != authority
- evidence sufficiency != authority
- authority != binding
- authority at one time != authority at another time
