# Convergence Lens

## Purpose

Inspect whether independent sources reach similar structural conclusions without relying on one another.

## Questions

- Which sources make related observations?
- Are they independent?
- Do they share a common upstream source?
- Are they repeating language or independently reconstructing the pattern?
- What part of the claim converges?
- What parts remain disputed?
- Is convergence evidence-based or popularity-based?
- Are multiple sources actually one source genealogy?
- Does the convergence survive domain changes?
- What counterexamples exist?

## Pressure Tests

- consensus != convergence
- repetition != independence
- many links != many sources
- shared conclusion != shared evidence
- popularity != validity
- cross-domain recurrence may strengthen a structural hypothesis but does not prove universality
