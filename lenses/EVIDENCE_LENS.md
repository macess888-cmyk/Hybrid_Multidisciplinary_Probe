# Evidence Lens

## Purpose

Inspect what evidence exists, what it supports, what it does not establish, and whether it can be independently examined.

## Questions

- What is the claim?
- What evidence supports it?
- What type of evidence is present?
- Is the evidence primary or secondary?
- Is the evidence complete, current, and relevant?
- Is provenance preserved?
- Can another person inspect or reproduce it?
- Does the evidence support the full claim or only a narrower version?
- What evidence is missing?
- What remains unknown?

## Pressure Tests

- record != evidence
- citation != verification
- evidence exists != evidence sufficient
- repeated claim != independent confirmation
- signed != true
- passing test != complete correctness
