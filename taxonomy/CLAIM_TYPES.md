# Claim Types

## Technical

Claims about implementation, software, hardware, protocols, algorithms, or engineering behavior.

---

## Architectural

Claims about system structure, relationships, boundaries, responsibilities, or composition.

---

## Scientific

Claims regarding hypotheses, theories, experiments, observations, or reproducible research.

---

## Operational

Claims about runtime behavior, deployment, maintenance, execution, or operational capability.

---

## Security

Claims regarding confidentiality, integrity, availability, authentication, authorization, resilience, or attack resistance.

---

## Governance

Claims regarding authority, responsibility, oversight, accountability, admissibility, or consequence management.

---

## Legal

Claims regarding legislation, regulation, compliance, liability, judicial findings, or legal authority.

---

## Policy

Claims describing organizational or governmental rules, procedures, standards, or guidance.

---

## Empirical

Claims supported by measurement, observation, experiment, or collected evidence.

---

## Historical

Claims describing past events or chronological developments.

---

## Priority

Claims asserting novelty, first discovery, uniqueness, or precedence.

---

## Marketing

Claims intended primarily to persuade, promote, advertise, or position products or organizations.

---

## Predictive

Claims about future outcomes, trends, risks, or expectations.

---

## Normative

Claims expressing how something ought to be rather than how it currently is.
