# Purpose

Define the standard evidence workflow used by the Hybrid Multidisciplinary Probe.

The objective is to preserve evidence lineage, architectural boundaries, reproducibility, and proportional conclusions while preventing unsupported inference.

Every campaign, regardless of subject, follows this same cycle.

# Core Principle

Observe first.

Conclude last.

Everything between must remain inspectable.

# Operational Rules

Every conclusion must reference admitted evidence.

Primary sources are preferred whenever available.

Secondary sources are explicitly identified.

Unknown remains UNKNOWN.

Evidence strength is recorded.

Architectural boundaries remain explicit.

Relationships are inspectable.

Claims remain proportional to available evidence.

---

# Evidence Philosophy

The observatory does not seek to prove predetermined conclusions.

It seeks to:

observe,

classify,

connect,

pressure-test,

and preserve uncertainty.

Claims expand only when evidence expands.

---

# Continuous Revalidation

Every observation remains revisitable.

New evidence may:

strengthen,

weaken,

replace,

or retire

previous conclusions.

Historical observations remain preserved.

Supersession never erases lineage.

---

# Completion Criteria

A campaign reaches maturity when:

Primary sources have been admitted.

Major entities have been identified.

Relationships have been mapped.

Claims have proportional evidence.

Questions are explicitly tracked.

Patterns emerge across independent observations.

Cross-campaign synthesis becomes possible.

Remaining uncertainty is documented.

---

# Observatory Commitment

Evidence before explanation.

Relationships before narratives.

Architecture before conclusions.

Inspection before interpretation.

Uncertainty preserved until evidence permits reduction.
