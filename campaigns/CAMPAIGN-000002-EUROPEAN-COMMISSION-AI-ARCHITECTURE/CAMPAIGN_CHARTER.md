# CAMPAIGN-000002 - European Commission AI Architecture

## Purpose

Map, inspect, and pressure-test the European Commission's public AI governance, research, funding, evaluation, regulatory, and institutional architecture.

## Root Source

European Commission public portal

https://commission.europa.eu/index_en

## Primary Questions

- Which Commission bodies hold responsibility for AI governance?
- Which documents are legally binding, advisory, technical, strategic, or promotional?
- Which branches concern model evaluation, safety, transparency, incident reporting, and human oversight?
- Which public sources bear directly on ITR-004 agent-behavior classification?
- Which claims are supported by primary EU documents?
- Which conclusions remain outside the available evidence?

## Campaign Posture

- root portal != primary evidence for every linked claim
- guidance != law
- strategy != operational capability
- institutional authority != technical correctness
- evaluation != execution authorization
- documented behavior != demonstrated intent
- UNKNOWN -> HOLD

## Scope

Included:

- European Commission pages
- European AI Office materials
- EUR-Lex documents
- Publications Office records
- CORDIS research results
- consultations
- funding and tender records
- official guidance
- technical reports
- linked primary research

Excluded:

- unsupported media summaries
- broad conclusions from navigation pages
- claims of intent without direct evidence
- technical certification without reproducible carriers
