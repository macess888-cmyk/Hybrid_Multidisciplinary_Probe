# CAMPAIGN-000001 - AI Governance Architectures

## Purpose

Observe, compare, pressure test, and connect public AI governance architectures across technical, institutional, governmental, security, operational, and scientific domains.

## Primary Question

Do emerging AI governance architectures preserve the distinctions required to move from reality and evidence to authorized execution and verified outcome without collapsing one layer into another?

## Campaign Posture

- observation before explanation
- evidence before conclusion
- no authority by reputation alone
- no layer inherits validity silently
- UNKNOWN -> HOLD
- broad observation remains separate from technical verification

## Candidate Domains

- information governance
- execution governance
- runtime governance
- admissibility
- authority continuity
- evidence integrity
- telemetry
- model evaluation
- cryptographic governance evidence
- continuous revalidation
- recovery
- remedy
- institutional AI
- government AI infrastructure
- cybersecurity
- quantum-safe communication
