# Campaign Scope

## Included

- public LinkedIn posts and articles
- government publications
- standards
- technical papers
- company architecture claims
- public proof surfaces
- GitHub repositories
- security advisories
- public comments that materially refine or challenge a claim
- source genealogy
- cross-domain convergence

## Excluded

- private or inaccessible material
- identity claims unsupported by public evidence
- confidential systems
- broad certification
- legal conclusions without legal authority
- technical PASS without an inspectable carrier
- universal conclusions from local examples

## Review Boundary

This campaign evaluates claims, evidence, architecture, boundaries, relationships, and open questions.

It does not certify the systems or authors being observed.
