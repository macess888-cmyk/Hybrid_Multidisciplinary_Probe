# Observatory Intelligence

## Purpose

The purpose of the observatory is not to answer questions.

Its purpose is to improve the quality of questions that survive observation.

Every conclusion remains proportional to available evidence.

Unknowns remain preserved.

---

## Intelligence Principles

Observation before explanation.

Evidence before conclusion.

Relationships before narratives.

Independent convergence before consensus.

Unknown before invention.

Reality remains upstream.

---

## Intelligence Invariant

No synthesis may silently manufacture evidence that the observations do not contain.
